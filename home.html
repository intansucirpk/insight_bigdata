Summary of the 4 main analytical methods used in this research

1. Order Priority Classification

The system categorizes transactions into High, Medium, and Low Priority. This helps identify the urgency of order processing across different regions.

2. 36-Month Sales Forecasting

The forecasting model predicts sales for the next 3 years. Regions with stable historical trends, such as Western Europe, show the most consistent predictions.

3. Customer Pattern Clustering

The clustering analysis groups regions based on similar purchasing behavior. This is useful for market segmentation and targeted marketing strategies.

4. Anomaly Detection

This model identifies unusual or irregular transactions, including sudden spikes or outliers. It serves as an important feature for monitoring and fraud prevention.
